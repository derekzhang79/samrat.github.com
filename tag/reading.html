Reading Packet

Not unlike to-do lists, reading lists(or rather read-later lists) always seem to grow at a way greater pace than a person can check-off its items. That's one of the reasons why I made Reading Packet, a small web app that uses the Pocket API to help you find articles depending on how much time you've got.

If you haven't started using a read-later app, Pocket is really great, and free! And once you've added a few items, you can start reading with Reading Packet whenever you're free.

Give it a try! and don't forget to tell me what you think.
